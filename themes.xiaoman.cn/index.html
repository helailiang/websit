About Us

SAINT CHEMICAL

SAINT is a professional manufacturer specializing in the production and supply of high-quality chemical products. We are committed to providing comprehensive chemical solutions including organic chemicals, synthetic resins, polyether polyols, synthetic rubber, synthetic fibers, and epoxy resins. Our product portfolio includes VAM (Vinyl Acetate Monomer), VAE Emulsion, PVA (Polyvinyl Alcohol), SEBS, SBS, SIS, and various specialty chemicals. With advanced manufacturing facilities and strict quality control systems, we serve customers worldwide across various industries including construction, automotive, textiles, packaging, and more. Our mission is to deliver innovative chemical solutions that meet the evolving needs of our global customers.
