About Us

LINYI PACKING MATERIALS

Is located in Linyi City, Shandong Province,China. The company is a manufacturer integrating industry and trade, specializing in the production of plastic flexible packaging. It mainly produces flexible packaging bags, vacuum packaging bags, paper shopping bags, handle bags, carrier bags,aluminum foil packaging bags, stand-up bags, zipper bags and packaging sheets and film rolls. The products are widely used in food, snacks,clothing, laundry, agrochemical and other packaging fields.
